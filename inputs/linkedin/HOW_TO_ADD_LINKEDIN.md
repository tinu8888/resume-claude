# Drop your LinkedIn profile PDF in this folder

How to get it:

1. Open your LinkedIn profile.
2. Click the "Resources" button (or "More") in your intro section, under your name.
3. Choose "Save to PDF".
4. Move the downloaded PDF into this folder (`inputs/linkedin/`).

Then run `/start` in Claude. It reads this PDF as the main source for your background.

You can delete this file. It's just a guide.
